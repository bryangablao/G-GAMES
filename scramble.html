How to Play:
- A scrambled word appears in the center.
- Type your guess in the input box.
- Press Spacebar to submit your guess (Check).
- Press Enter to get the next scrambled word (Next Word).
- Click Hint or press + (Numpad) to reveal the first letter.
- Click Surrender or press Delete to reveal the correct word.
- Press Tab to toggle the help text.
- Press Esc to go back to the lobby.
- The game shows helpful messages on the scoreboard instead of counters.
- Achievements unlock automatically when you guess words correctly in a streak!
