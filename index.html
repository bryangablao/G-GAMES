About G-GAMES

G-GAMES is an interactive online mini-games lobby featuring a 5x5 grid of square game cards. Each card is a single-player mini-game, like Sudoku, Tic-Tac-Toe, Snake, Flappy Bird, Rock-Paper-Scissors, and more.

The site is fully responsive, playable on desktop, tablet, and mobile, with a starry night background and flickering stars to create a lively atmosphere.

This platform is open to the public, allowing anyone to play, but each game is designed for one player at a time. The site is created for fun, casual gaming, accessible to all.
